Sajjan Raj Vaidya

        Sajjan Raj Vaidya (Nepali: सज्जन राज वैद्य) is a Nepali Singer 
        and full Stack Web Developer. He is a symphony of expressive vocals, 
        intricate metaphors and anomalous song structures arriving at the 
        crescendo as a complete musical journey.[1] And rose to prominence 
        after the release of his singles "Hawaijahaj" (2016)[2][3][4] and 
        "Chitthi Bhitra" (2018).[2][5] One of his most widely known singles
